Dawn

This is the time when the sky starts to lighten in the morning

Sunrise

The time in the morning when the sun starts to rise in the sky.

Noon

Noon time is typically 12:00 PM or 12:00 in the 24-hour clock. It is the middle of the day.

Evening

Evening is the period of a day that begins at end of afternoon and overlaps with night.

Sunset

the disappearance of the Sun at the end of the Sun path.
